FAQ

What is CineDraft?
CineDraft is a fantasy film league app where you draft movies and compete with friends based on real critic and audience ratings from Letterboxd, IMDb, and TMDB.
How do I create or join a league?
After signing up, tap "Create League" to start a new league and invite friends via a share link, or tap "Join League" and enter an invite code.
How is scoring calculated?
Movie scores are a weighted average of Letterboxd (60%), IMDb (20%), and TMDB (20%) ratings, normalized to a 100-point scale.
How do I report a bug?
Email us at cinedraftapp@gmail.com with a description of the issue and any screenshots if possible.
Is CineDraft free?
Yes! CineDraft is free to download and play.
