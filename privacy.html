Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

CineDraft ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Cody Wittlinger. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use CineDraft.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the app:

  • Account Information: Email address and user ID when you create an account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile Information: Display name, bio, location, tagline, profile photo, and banner image that you choose to provide.
  • User Content: Movie reviews, ratings, thoughts/remarks, watchlists, favorites, and custom lists you create within the app.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as movies you watch, rate, or add to lists.
  • Crash Data: Crash logs collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and fix bugs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality, including your profile, leagues, drafts, and social features.
  • To display your activity and content to other users within the app (e.g., league members, followers).
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Storage, and Crashlytics). Data is transmitted over encrypted connections. Some data is also cached locally on your device using encrypted storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, data storage, crash reporting.
  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — Movie information, posters, and trailers. CineDraft uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
  • MDBList / OMDb — Aggregated movie ratings and awards data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your data is only shared as necessary to operate the app through the third-party services listed above.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

CineDraft is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can also update or remove most of your data directly within the app's settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
