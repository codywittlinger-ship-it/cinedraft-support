1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CineDraft, operated by Quiet Craft ("we", "us", or "the App"), collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our movie social platform — including fantasy film leagues, Oscar prediction pools, movie reviews, thoughts, lists, journals, and social features.

By creating an account and using CineDraft, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication and account recovery)
  • Display name

  • Bio, location, and profile customization

Movie Activity Data

  • Movie reviews, ratings, and thoughts (short-form discussion posts)
  • Movie lists (personal and collaborative)

League & Draft Data

  • League memberships, names, and settings

  • Draft schedules and availability responses
  • Team rosters and standings

Oscar Prediction Data

  • Oscar prediction pool memberships
  • Category predictions and sealed ballots
  • Prediction scores and leaderboard standings

Social & Communication Data

  • Chat messages within leagues
  • Direct messages and group messages between users
  • Trade proposals and trade history
  • Following and followers relationships
  • Blocked users list
  • Content reports and support requests submitted through the app
  • Privacy preference settings

Usage & Analytics Data

  • Device information (type, OS version, unique identifiers)
  • App interactions and feature usage
  • Session timestamps and duration

Third-Party Account Data

Achievement & Statistics Data

  • Draft performance and win/loss records

Preferences & Settings

  • App customization preferences

  • Enable communication between users (league chat, direct messages, group messages)

  • Track achievements, stats, and player performance

  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to your support requests

Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we process your personal data based on:

  • Performance of a contract (operating your account and the App)
  • Legitimate interests (improving features, preventing abuse, analytics)
  • Consent (where required for notifications or optional features)
  • Legal obligations (complying with applicable laws)

4. Your Privacy Controls

CineDraft gives you control over your privacy. You can manage these settings in your Profile Settings:

Profile Visibility

  • Public: Anyone can view your profile and stats
  • Friends Only: Only league members can view your profile
  • Private: Only you can see your full profile

Trade Requests

  • Everyone: Anyone can send you trade proposals
  • League Members Only: Only people in your leagues can propose trades
  • No One: Disable incoming trade requests

Direct Messages

  • Everyone: Anyone can message you
  • League Members Only: Only people in your leagues can message you
  • No One: Disable direct messages

Activity Sharing

  • Your movie activity (watched, rated, reviewed) can be shared to the social feed
  • You can control which activity types are auto-shared in your privacy settings
  • Drafted movies are only visible to members of your league

Blocking Users

You can block users to prevent them from viewing your profile, sending messages, or proposing trades. Blocked users cannot see your activity in shared leagues.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data in the following limited circumstances:

With Other Users

  • League members can see your display name, profile picture, league-specific stats, and chat messages within shared leagues
  • Followers and friends can see your activity in the social feed (based on your sharing settings)
  • Users can see information based on your privacy settings (see Section 4)
  • Commissioners can see league management data for leagues they administrate
  • Trade partners can see proposed trade details
  • Collaborative list members can see and edit shared list content

With Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google) for hosting, database, authentication, cloud storage, and push notifications
  • Service providers operate under strict confidentiality obligations and may only use data to provide services to us

For Legal Reasons

  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • To investigate and prevent fraud, security issues, or technical problems
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)

Aggregated & Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you for analytics, research, and improving the App.

6. Data Retention

Active Accounts

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the App's services.

Deleted Accounts

  • When you delete your account, your personal data is scheduled for permanent deletion within 30 days
  • During this 30-day period, you may recover your account by contacting support
  • Some data may be retained longer if required by law or to resolve disputes

League Data

  • Active league data is retained indefinitely
  • Inactive league data (no activity for 3 years) is automatically deleted
  • If you leave a league, your picks and messages in that league remain visible to other members

Messages & Chat

  • League chat messages are retained as long as the league exists
  • Direct messages are retained until deleted by you or account deletion

Analytics Data

Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase Authentication with secure token management
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications to keep you informed about:

  • Draft start times and when it's your turn to pick
  • Trade proposals and acceptances
  • League invitations and updates
  • Direct messages
  • Important app announcements

You can manage notification preferences in your device settings or within the App. We use Firebase Cloud Messaging, which may collect device tokens and delivery data.

9. Widgets & Live Activities

CineDraft offers Home Screen widgets and Lock Screen Live Activities to keep you updated:

  • Widgets display movie data (trending movies, watchlist, unread messages) on your Home Screen
  • Live Activities show draft countdown timers on your Lock Screen during active drafts
  • Widget and Live Activity data is stored locally on your device and refreshed periodically
  • No additional personal data is collected through widgets or Live Activities beyond what the App already collects

12. Third-Party Services

CineDraft integrates with third-party services to provide functionality. These services have their own privacy policies:

Firebase (Google Cloud)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user sign-in and authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores league, draft, and user data
  • Firebase Cloud Storage: Stores profile images and banners
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Processes backend logic
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Delivers push notifications
  • Firebase Analytics: Tracks app usage and performance
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects crash reports and diagnostic data to improve stability

Apple Sign-In

Allows secure authentication using your Apple ID. Apple may share your email and name with us (with your permission).

Google Sign-In

Allows secure authentication using your Google account. Google may share your email and name with us.

The Movie Database (TMDB)

Provides movie data, images, metadata, and streaming availability (via JustWatch). If you connect your TMDB account, an OAuth access token is stored securely on your device to sync your ratings, watchlist, and favorites. We do not send other personal information to TMDB.

MDBList

Provides aggregated movie ratings from multiple sources. We send movie identifiers (not personal data) to retrieve rating information.

Does The Dog Die

Provides content warnings and trigger information for movies. We send movie identifiers (not personal data) to retrieve content warning data.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. Please review their policies before using their services.
